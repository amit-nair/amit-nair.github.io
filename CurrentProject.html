How Do Animals Move In Groups?

Working under: Prof. Dr. Tomohiro Nagashima & Dr. Man Su

A self-paced open-world digital learning environment

This project explores the design and evaluation of an open-world, simulation-based learning environment for teaching complex systems concepts, such as emergent behavior and non-linearity. The environment features a 3D open-world map where learners use a controllable virtual character to interact with scaffolded instructional content and an LLM-powered pedagogical tutor.
